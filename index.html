Our Vision

Our vision is a future where Persons with Disabilities have equal access to opportunities, resources, and social connections through innovative technology.

Our Mission

To develop and implement innovative assistive technologies that empower PWDs, fostering independence and improving quality of life.

What's New

Check out our latest features designed to empower our users.

Visual Scheduler

Organize daily routines with a customizable, picture-based schedule.

Text-to-Vibration

Convert text into vibration patterns for tactile communication.

AAC Communication Board

Empowers non-verbal users to communicate with picture-based tiles.

Braille Learning Tool

An interactive tool to learn and practice Braille with instant feedback.

Lip-Reading Assistant

An AI-powered tool to help users practice and improve lip-reading skills.

Sign Language Translator

A real-time translator for converting sign language into text or speech.
